Located in Gujarat, Dwarkadhish Temple, also known as Jagat Mandir, is among the oldest temples dedicated to Lord Krishna. With a five-story structure supported by 72 pillars, it stands as a marvel of architecture. Believed to be over 2,500 years old, the temple attracts millions of devotees every year and is one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites.
