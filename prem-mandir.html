Prem Mandir is located in the divine town of Vrindavan and is one of the latest temples dedicated to Lord Krishna. The temple was founded by spiritual Guru Kripalu Maharaj. Built with stunning white marble, the temple spans across 54 acres. The walls and structure depict various key events from Lord Krishna's life, making it not only a spiritual but also an educational site.
