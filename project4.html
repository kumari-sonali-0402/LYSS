10 Most Popular Temples in India of Lord Krishna for Krishna Devotees

Lord Krishna is said to be one of the greatest philosophers of the world and one of the incarnations of Lord Vishnu. He is worshiped in the whole world, and there are numerous Krishna temples across the globe. These temples are popular due to their association with Lord Krishna. The spiritual aura of these Krishna temples has made them calm pilgrimage spots for the worshipers. India is home to many beautiful Krishna temples that are known for being architectural marvels. Read this article to know about the most amazing temples of Lord Krishna.

1. Prem Mandir, Vrindavan

Prem Mandir is located in the divine town of Vrindavan and is one of the latest temples dedicated to Lord Krishna. The temple was founded by spiritual Guru Kripalu Maharaj. The main structure of the temple is built with white marbles and looks stunning. This is mainly a spiritual and educational site that spans 54 acres. Idols of Lord Krishna and his followers depict various key events in the main temple.

2. ISKCON Temple, Multiple Locations

ISKCON is a global movement with temples across the world. The International Society for Krishna Consciousness promotes the teachings of Lord Krishna. There are many ISKCON temples in India, such as in Delhi, Mumbai, Vrindavan, Bangalore, Kolkata, Ahmedabad, Baroda, and more. These temples are well-maintained and visited by devotees from different religions and backgrounds.

3. Dwarkadhish Temple, Dwarka

Dwarkadhish Temple is located in the holy city of Dwarka in the state of Gujarat. It is a famous pilgrimage site for devotees. This Hindu temple is dedicated to Lord Krishna and consists of a 5-story structure supported by 72 pillars. This temple is also known as Jagat Mandir and is believed to be around 2,500 years old according to the Archaeological Survey of India.

4. Guruvayur Sri Krishna Temple, Guruvayur

Guruvayur Temple, located in Kerala, is also known as the Bhuloka Vaikunta. The main deity of the temple is Lord Vishnu, whose incarnation as Lord Krishna is worshiped here. It is famous as the Dwarka of South India. The temple is considered one of the most admired temples in the country, and it is believed that Lord Brahma himself worshipped the idol of Lord Krishna here.

5. Jugal Kishore Temple, Mathura

Located in the holy town of Mathura, Jugal Kishore Temple is a peaceful place of worship. It is one of the oldest and most popular Krishna temples in Mathura. The temple is also known as Kesi Ghata and is constructed using red sandstone. The main entrance of the temple is located at the eastern end, and it is a serene place for devotees to find solace.
